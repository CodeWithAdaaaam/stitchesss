Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

How long does a custom order take?+

Most custom pieces take 2–3 weeks to hand-stitch, depending on complexity. Rush orders may be possible — ask us on WhatsApp.

How much does a custom piece cost?+

Pricing depends on size, material and detail. Bags typically start at 1,800 MAD; gloves and accessories from 650 MAD. We'll quote you directly after your DM.

Do you ship internationally?+

Yes — we ship worldwide from our atelier in Morocco, with tracked delivery. Shipping time is typically 5–10 business days after completion.

Can I choose my own colors?+

Always. Every custom order starts with your palette — send us a reference photo or describe the mood you want.

Home/Who We Are
Est. On The Atlantic Coast

A maison built on patience, not production.

Maison Of Stitches began with a single hook, a spool of cotton, and a conviction: that luxury should be measured in hours, not units. Today, our artisans work from a small atelier facing the Atlantic, where the rhythm of the tide sets the rhythm of the work.

Every bag, glove and accessory that leaves our hands is 100% handmade — no molds, no machines, no two pieces ever quite alike.

What We Stand For

Handmade

Made in Morocco

Our Values

Slowness, as a form of respect.

We believe a garment made slowly carries something a machine cannot replicate — intention. We limit our runs, refuse to rush an order, and would rather disappoint a deadline than a stitch.

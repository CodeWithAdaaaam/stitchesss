Our Story

Not just a product — a handmade ritual.

Chapter I

The Craft

It begins with a single hook and a spool of raw cotton. There is no pattern printed on paper — only a rhythm passed down, hand to hand, counted stitch by stitch until the shape appears.

Chapter II

The Coast

Our atelier sits where the desert meets the Atlantic. The salt air, the light, the slow pace of a fishing town — it all finds its way into the work, into the colors we choose and the patience we keep.

Chapter III

The Hands

Behind every piece is a name, a family, a lineage of women who have stitched for generations. When you hold a Maison Of Stitches piece, you are holding their hands too.

“We do not make things quickly. We make them well — and let the tide set the pace.”

Founder, Maison Of Stitches
